Hi! I'm Tanvi, a creative technologist currently based in NYC. My creative practice is centered around building games, interactive installations, and weird web experiences primarily as educational escapades.

Recently, I've been experimenting with climate science data under the guise of a game-maker working on a multiplayer game: Tridal. I am a Rhizome Microgrants 2023 recipient and has spoken about my work at Rhizome, Claremont Colleges, and Parsons School of Design.
