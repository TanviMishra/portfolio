I am Tanvi Mishra, a [designer + technologist] who occasionally builds cool stuff. I work with emergent technologies through interactive installations, games, and weird web projects.

I believe that design visualises possibility. My work presents alternate possibilities of our lives allowing viewers a moment of respite and/or reflection.

Currently, I am an undergrad student @ Parsons School of Design in NYC studying Design and Technology. I have 5 years freelance experience in game development, graphic design, and branding.
